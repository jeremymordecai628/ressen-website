Academics

Over the years, the school has placed itself on the academic map within the Trans Nzioa County, with key milestones achieved in that short period. The first national exam of K.C.P.E year 2018, had a mean mark of 323 out of the possible 500.The same good performance has been maintained with leading students scoring over 400 marks and with an improvement mean of 344 marks in year 2022 K.C.P.E examinations. The school has hired competent teachers as the director being educationists, she knows the caliber of teachers to have and ensures they are well motivated for good outcomes.

Co-curricular

All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy, at Ressen Gardens Education Centre, students are also involved in various co-curriculum activities to ensure we produce all rounded personalities . We have an extensive playground where the kids play and have a good time while also learning social and interpersonal skills. The students engage in a diverse range of co-curricular activities, including the art of music, games and debates. The Centre provides access to a well-equipped music room, and proficient instructors are available to guide students in playing various musical instruments and developing their vocal abilities. The school also participates and shines in Music festivals where they have won several accolades
